This Privacy Policy describes how Conduit Lab LLC ("Company," "we," "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use ClauseCheck ("the Service"). We take the privacy of your legal documents seriously and have designed the Service with that in mind.

2. How we handle your contract documents

We understand that the contracts you upload may contain sensitive business and legal information. Here is exactly how we handle them:

  • Standard tier: Contract text is transmitted securely to our Cloudflare Worker, passed to the Anthropic API for analysis, and not persistently stored. The text exists in memory only during processing.
  • Pro tier (The Vault): Documents you explicitly choose to save are stored encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption in Supabase Storage. Only your account can access them. You can delete them at any time.
  • We do not use your documents to train AI models. Contract text is passed to the Anthropic API for inference only, under Anthropic's API terms which prohibit training on API inputs.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your contract documents with any third party except as necessary to provide the Service (Anthropic API for analysis).

3. How we use your information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Service
  • To authenticate your identity and enforce account security
  • To process payments and manage your subscription
  • To send transactional emails (signup confirmation, trial expiry notice, payment receipts)
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent fraud and abuse
  • To comply with legal obligations

We do not use your information for advertising, and we do not sell your personal data to any third party.

5. Data retention

  • Account data: Retained while your account is active and for 30 days after deletion.
  • Contract text (Standard tier): Not retained after analysis is complete.
  • Vault documents (Pro tier): Retained until you delete them or close your account.
  • Analysis history: Retained while your account is active. Deleted when you close your account.
  • Billing records: Retained for 7 years as required by applicable tax laws.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for documents stored in The Vault
  • Row-level security in our database ensuring each user can only access their own data
  • Single-device session enforcement to prevent unauthorized account access
  • API keys stored as encrypted secrets, never exposed in client-side code

No system is perfectly secure. If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to [email protected].

7. Attorney-client privilege

We are aware that legal professionals using ClauseCheck may be reviewing documents subject to attorney-client privilege. By using the Service, you acknowledge that transmitting documents to a third-party AI service may have implications for privilege, and you accept responsibility for making that determination under the applicable rules of professional conduct in your jurisdiction. We recommend reviewing your state bar's guidance on cloud services and AI tools before uploading privileged materials.
